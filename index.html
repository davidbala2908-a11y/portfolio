About

I design interfaces by starting with the people who'll use them, not the screen. My process runs the full length of a UX practice — user research, personas, journey mapping, information architecture, wireframing — through to polished, high-fidelity prototypes in Figma.

My flagship project, an AI-powered farming app, asked me to design for smallholder farmers making high-stakes decisions with low digital literacy. That constraint shaped everything: research that listened before it assumed, and interfaces built for clarity over cleverness.

Certified in UI/UX Design through Login360 Institute, Chennai — currently building out a portfolio of mobile and web case studies.

How I work
